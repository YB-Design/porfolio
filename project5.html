RAJA CASABLANCA CLUB ATHLETIC

Rebranding

Description:

A Fusion of Legacy and Modernity. In the pursuit of rebranding, I embarked on a journey to capture the essence of Raja Casablanca’s rich history while infusing a contemporary energy. Carefully curated, the new badge stands as a tribute to tradition. Guided by the iconic emblem of the past, I seamlessly integrated the emblematic eagle into a sleek linear design. Enhancing the badge’s visual prowess, I strategically positioned the ball at the eagle’s feet, injecting dynamic movement. The team’s spirit found its reflection as I elegantly curved the text beneath the eagle, reinforcing the essence. A triumphant touch emerged with the introduction of three stars, a proud symbol of the team’s historic achievements, adorning the eagle’s chest. This reimagined badge masterfully bridges the past and present.
