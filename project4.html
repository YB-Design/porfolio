ÉQUIPE QUÉBEC

Branding

Description:

Embarking on the logo design for a national hockey team was a journey brimming with cultural symbolism and historical homage. The choice of the majestic snowy owl, a revered emblem of Quebec, set the stage for a narrative weaving diverse threads of heritage. Atop the owl’s noble visage, the white pine stood as a poignant tribute to First Nations, whispering of resilience and ancestral roots. A hidden layer unfurled, revealing a nod to the French influence shaping Quebec’s identity. The steadfast shield cradled by the owl represented guardianship of the French language—a heritage guarded and celebrated. Envisioned within this emblem was harmonious synergy, a fusion of cultures and histories. The hockey stick, forming the letter “Q,” denoted Quebec and symbolized the team’s vigor, unity, and pursuit of victory.
